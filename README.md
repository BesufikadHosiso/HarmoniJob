## 🎯 The Mission
In a market saturated with generic applications, **HarmoniJob** helps you cut through the noise. By implementing the **R.I.C.E. (Reward, Ideology, Coercion, Ego)** framework, this tool allows you to stop "applying" and start "resonating." We don't just format your resume; we align your unique technical history with the core psychological needs of the company.

> "The difference between an invisible applicant and a top 1% candidate isn't just the skill—it's the ability to demonstrate that you understand the specific value you bring to the company's unique mission."

---

## 🚀 Core Features

* **Job-Fit Gap Analysis:** Compare your profile against any Job Description (JD) to identify the "bridge" between your current experience and their requirements.
* **R.I.C.E. Resume Writer:** An AI engine that rewrites your experience bullets, shifting from "duty-based" (boring) to "impact-based" (high-signal) narratives.
* **CEO-Level Interview Coach:** An interactive simulator that triggers "active recall," teaching you how to "pivot and connect" when faced with technical gaps.
* **Strategic Profile Management:** A centralized hub for your projects and skills that ensures consistency across every customized application.

## 🛠 The Tech Stack
We built HarmoniJob using a lean, high-performance, and **entirely free** tech stack:

* **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (Frontend & API)
* **Database:** [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) (PostgreSQL & Auth)
* **AI Engine:** [Google Gemini API](https://aistudio.google.com/)
* **UI/UX:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) & [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/)
* **Deployment:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
